Iron Ore

Iron ore is a mineral block found underground. It is the most common mineral that can be used to make tools and armor.

Gold Ore

Gold ore is one of the rarest types of mineral blocks found underground.

Redstone Ore

Redstone ore is the ore block from which redstone is obtained.

Lapis Ore

Lapis lazuli ore is the ore block from which lapis lazuli is obtained.

Iron Ingot

Iron ingots are versatile metal crafting ingredients.

Gold Ingot

Gold ingots are a metal that is used to craft the second tier of armor, and the first of tools.

Diamond Gem

Diamonds are an uncommon material. They are mainly used to craft the highest tier of tools and armor, and the enchantment table.

Emerald Gem

Emeralds are an uncommon material, and are the currency used in villager trading.
